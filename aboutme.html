I'm a mom, college graduate, and a late-blooming book lover. During my undergraduate and graduate years, I read so many textbooks and papers that I developed a bit of a resistance to reading, even reading for fun. After graduating in 2016, it took me years before I picked up books regularly purely for enjoyment again.

Now as a mom to two young kids, I'm rediscovering the joy of reading. It's not only a great way to unwind, but it also helps me connect with friends through shared book interests and engaging conversations. I mostly listen to audiobooks through Libby, allowing me to multitask while doing my usual stuff like driving kids around, cooking, working out, or tidying up.
