Hi, I'm Nicole Ray

I'm a mom and college graduate who didn't start reading for fun until last year. Graduate school sucked the life out of me and I only read textbooks. I am married and have two little kids. I have found that I enjoy many genres of books and I'm excited to explore more.

My favorite genre is nonfiction but I am starting to branch out. I generally listen on Libby through my library so I can double task while doing things like driving, dishes, or cleaning. I rarely read a hard copy book but find it pretty fun to do at the pool in the summer or at the beach at least.
